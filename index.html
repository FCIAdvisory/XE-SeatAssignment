Framing the challenge

This is not just a seating exercise.

It is an operating model question expressed through physical space.

Good space planning helps leaders make clear decisions about proximity, privacy, collaboration, growth, and fairness.

Desired outcome

A practical workplace model leadership can use beyond the initial move.

Operational alignment

Teams are placed based on how work actually gets done.

Clear governance

Office and workstation decisions are based on consistent criteria.

Growth capacity

The model can absorb hiring, restructuring, and expansion.

Employee clarity

People understand the why behind assignments and changes.
